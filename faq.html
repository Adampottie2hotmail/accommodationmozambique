Good to Know

Check‑in / Check‑out
Check‑in from 2pm • Check‑out by 10am. Flexible when possible.
House Rules
No smoking indoors. No parties or events. Quiet hours 8pm-7am.
Cancellation
Full refund up to 30 days before arrival.
Accessibility
Unit 15 is wheelchair accesible. Please ask if you have specific needs.
How Secure is the Lodge?
Completely secure. There are security guards on duty, night and day. Your security is guaranteed. The Cottages may be left open, unsecured, day and night
Do I need a 4x4?
Yes, you need a 4 X 4. Transfers are available from Inhambane airport (an hours drive away), or a secured carpark around an hour drive away. 4 X 4 rental is also available, please ask for further details
How Safe is Mozambique?
Mozambique is completely secure. There were minor protests after the elections in October 2024 for three months. Most big cities in USA are far more dangerous than anywhere I Mozambique. Come and enjoy the peace and tranquility.
What Activities are there?
There is a range of activities including, hiking, 4 x 4 trails, beach fishing, deep sea charters, whale watching (seasonal), ocean boating experiences, bird watching, snorkeling, swimming, kayaking in the lake. Jetskiing and waterskiing in the lake are very popular, however you will need to have your own jetski and boat. Also available are dhow outings at Inhambane or a yachting experience in Bara.
Are Cellphone signal and Wi-Fi facilities available?
Yes, on request you may rent starlink.
